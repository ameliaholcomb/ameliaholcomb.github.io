Depth-Assisted Segmentation for Forest Carbon Inventories

Amelia Holcomb · Bill Tong · Megan Penny · Srinivasan Keshav

My master's thesis focused on the use of mobile phones to assist in carbon sequestration accounting. As part of this work, I developed a novel algorithm to derive tree diamater from a single image with depth and RGB channels.

I presented this work as a poster at Mobisys 2021, where it won the Best Poster Award. The poster can be downloaded here.

If you're brave enough to read the whole thesis, it can be found on UWSpace.
